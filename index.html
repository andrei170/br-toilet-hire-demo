Do you cover events as well as construction sites?

Yes, both. We've supplied facilities for organised events like Race Nation, Castle Runs and Runthrough, as well as ongoing construction site hire.

What's the difference between standard and luxury units?

Standard portable toilets are built for reliability on site. Luxury trailers add AC, running water and finishes suited to weddings and premium events.

Do you offer accessible units?

Yes, regulation-compliant accessible toilets are available alongside our standard range.

What areas do you cover?

Newport and South Wales as our base, with delivery available throughout Wales and the wider UK.
